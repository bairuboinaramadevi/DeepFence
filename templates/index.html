What Is DeepFence?

DeepFence is an advanced, AI-powered security framework that leverages Google's Agentic technologies to proactively protect AI and Machine Learning models, especially those used in defense. It dynamically defends against sophisticated threats like adversarial attacks and data poisoning, ensuring the integrity, privacy, and trustworthiness of critical AI systems.

How It Works

DeepFence functions as a multi-layered security framework, deploying 25 specialized AI agents leveraging Google ADK across all types of ML models (traditional, CNNs, and LLMs). It actively protects models and data through techniques like encrypted storage, password protection, and real-time alerts for unauthorized usage.

DeepFence to the Rescue!

DeepFence rescues data and models by proactively defending against modern threats traditional methods miss. It uses Google's agentic AI to deploy intelligent agents that autonomously detect, learn from, and neutralize adversarial attacks and data compromises in real-time. This ensures the integrity, privacy, and trustworthiness of critical AI models, particularly in demanding defense applications.
